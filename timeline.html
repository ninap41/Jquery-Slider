  • 1984

    First Scholarship Awarded
    The Horatio Alger Association determines that the provision of college scholarships should be part its mission, and establishes the Horatio Alger National Scholarship Program for young people who are facing adversities. Scholarships valued at $5,000 each are awarded to seven students.

  • 1988

    National Scholarship Selection Committee
    The National Scholarship Selection Committee is created and chaired by Dr. John O. Hershey ('81). Dr. Hershey serves as the committee chairman for more than 18 years.

  • 1989

    National Scholars Conference
    The first National Scholars Conference, a series of educational and college preparatory sessions, is held in conjunction with the annual Awards events.

  • 1995

    National Youth Survey
    The Horatio Alger Association sponsors a national survey among teenagers to learn about their thoughts, concerns and motivations. The State of Our Nation's Youth surveys continue to be conducted every four years so that the Association can stay informed about issues affecting our nation's young people.
